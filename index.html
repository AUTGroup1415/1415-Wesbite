Cloud Computing Impact on Society

Cloud computing allows the industry to utilize the internet for computing services such as networking, software, analytics, and intelligence, to produce more efficient and economical ways of doing tasks. But one of the highest usages of cloud computing in society and the industry is data storage. Big companies and people together, hold so much sensitive data that its imperative that the cloud has a reliable and secure storage system. Security then become a huge factor as lots of sensitive data is being held somewhere across the globe. How is the data safely stored in the data centers, and also what security measures do companies do to protect this data.
